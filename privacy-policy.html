Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

2. Information We Collect

When you place an order or contact us, we collect:

  • Your name, phone number and WhatsApp number

We do not collect payment card details. All payments are processed directly via UPI or bank transfer, which you initiate on your own device.

3. How We Use Your Information

We do not sell, share or rent your personal information to any third party.

Orders are processed via WhatsApp. By submitting an order, you consent to us communicating with you on the number provided for the purpose of fulfilling your order. We do not add you to any broadcast list or send promotional messages without your explicit consent.

5. Data Retention
