From Cherry to Cup

No Shortcuts,
No Intermediaries

Every step from cherry to cup happens on our land in Coorg.

01

Hand Harvested

Every cherry is selectively hand-picked at peak ripeness by workers who have known this land for generations.

02

Sun Dried

Cherries are sun-dried after harvest. This natural drying process preserves the character of the bean before roasting.

03

Estate Roasted

Small-batch roasting on the farm. Each roast profile developed to honour the bean's natural character and origin.

04

Dispatched Fresh

Roasted and ground to order, sealed immediately, and handed to India Post within 48 hours for delivery across India.
